Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how PlatAlgo collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your username, email address (when using OAuth), and trading settings. We also collect usage data such as signal logs and command history to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the PlatAlgo Relay service, authenticate your identity, route trading signals to your MetaTrader 5 broker, and send service notifications you have opted into.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account credentials are stored with industry-standard security practices. MT5 broker passwords are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. API keys are stored as one-way hashes and cannot be recovered — only reset.

4. Data We Do Not Store

We do not store your MT5 broker passwords in plaintext. We do not have access to your trading account funds. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google and Facebook OAuth for optional authentication. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not receive your social media passwords — only a verified identity token.

6. Data Retention

Signal logs and command history are retained for operational purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support through the dashboard.

7. Cookies

We use session cookies to maintain your login state on the web dashboard. These cookies are HTTP-only and expire with your browser session unless you choose "Remember me".

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the PlatAlgo dashboard.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the PlatAlgo dashboard.
